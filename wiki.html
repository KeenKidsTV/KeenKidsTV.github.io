About the Show

Keen Kids is a 2D stop motion show about three boys — Max, Drew, and Tilo. They might look like your average school kids… but don’t let that fool you. These three cause pure mayhem. From running underground school businesses to accidentally committing crimes — they do it all. Join them on their wild, ridiculous, and totally unapproved adventures.

Main Characters

Max

The smart one — always coming up with big ideas, most of which go very wrong.

Drew

The wildcard — loud, fearless, and sometimes makes everything 100 times worse (in the best way).

Tilo

The quiet brainiac who secretly fuels most of the chaos behind the scenes.
